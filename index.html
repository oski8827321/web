The Science Behind

Understanding relativity, gravity, and time dilation

Time Dilation

Time passes differently in strong gravitational fields. The closer you are to a massive object like a black hole, the slower time moves relative to an outside observer.

Δt' = Δt√(1 - 2GM/rc²)

Gravitational Effects

🌍

Massive objects warp spacetime, creating what we experience as gravity. This warping affects both space and time dimensions.

Orbital Mechanics
